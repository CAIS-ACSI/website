Editorial team

Editor

Heather Hill
PhD, University of Western Ontario

Dr. Hill is an Associate Professor at the University of Western Ontario. She earned her MLIS and PhD in Information Science and Learning Technologies at the University of Missouri. She started at the University of Western Ontario in 2009 and obtained tenure in 2015. Her research interests centre on public libraries and fringe publishing. Dr. Hill has reviewed manuscripts for such journals as The International Journal of Information, Diversity, & Inclusion, The Library Quarterly, The Journal of Librarianship & Information Science, and the Journal of Web Librarianship. She was the president of CAIS from 2018–2019 and co-organized the Association of Library & Information Science Education conference in 2017.
