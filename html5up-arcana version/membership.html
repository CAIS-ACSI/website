Join our community

One of the core values that CAIS promotes is accessibility, so most of our services, events, and publications are not behind a paywall.

So why become a member then?
1) Because you see value in our mission and wish to contribute to its realisation.
2) Because only members can nominate people for CAIS awards.
3) Because members can attend the annual conference for free.*

*Registration fees of the annual conference are the same as the membership fees, so conference participation comes with a membership valid until the end of the year.
