Join the community

Become a member today. It's a great way to show your colors and support Canadian LIS scholarship.

Enrolled in a PhD or Master LIS or IS program in Canada? Claim your free membership

Get involved

CAIS is a community effort with many opportunities to contribute. You can join our team volunteers, check out open positions, or contact us.

Engage

Included in the membership is an access to the CAIS Slack, where you can discuss with colleagues, share news, create your own private channels for specific discussions and collaborations.

Make sure to also check out content, webinars, and other events on the CAIS community platform
